Who are the classes for?

Anyone who is keen to learn Italian or English, or improve their skills in the language.

How long will this take?

Classes last from 15 to 60 mins, according to your level and needs.
We offer classes over 30 days, from April 1st to April 30th

What will I learn in 30 days?

If you are a beginner or elementary level, you will be assigned some daily tasks that will take you 10/15 mins to complete over 30 days. You will be learning the basics of the language.

If you are an intermediate level you will find a place to boost your conversation skills.
